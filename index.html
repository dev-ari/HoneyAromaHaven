About The Family

How We Got Here

Honey Aroma Haven was founded by Adrianna S., a 24 y.o. web developer in April of 2020 after noticing the unhealthy impacts of too much social distance. Starting as an idea to create a safe place for herself and others, the purpose was to enjoy soothing tea while working while also allowing a healthy portion of social activity.

Needed for so many of us is the availability for many small yet important life elements that just don't fit in 24 hours. All in one place at one time. This was the plan... The truth is some of us love connection, some of us love distance, some of us love technology, and some of us love tea. Why not have it all and more?
